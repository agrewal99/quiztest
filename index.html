PHM 383 Quality Improvement and Implementation Science Quiz

1. What is the primary focus of Quality Improvement?

Correct Answer: 3. Quality Improvement focuses on context-dependent and local solutions to address specific problems.

2. Which model is commonly used in Quality Improvement initiatives?

Correct Answer: 3. Lean Six Sigma is commonly used in Quality Improvement to enhance efficiency by reducing waste and variability.

3. What is a key characteristic of Implementation Science?

Correct Answer: 3. Implementation Science involves methodical design and evaluation to understand the general benefits of an intervention.

4. Which component is part of the COM-B model?

Correct Answer: 1. The COM-B model includes components such as Capability (Physical), Motivation, and Opportunity.

5. What is the approach used by Quality Improvement to evaluate interventions?

Correct Answer: 2. Quality Improvement uses rapid reevaluation and iteration to adapt and improve processes quickly.

6. Which framework is NOT associated with Quality Improvement?

Correct Answer: 3. CFIR (Consolidated Framework for Implementation Research) is used in Implementation Science, not typically in Quality Improvement.
